# ChapSmart — Bitcoin to M-Pesa

Convert your Lightning sats to Tanzanian Shillings and send directly to any Vodacom M-Pesa number.

Enter a phone number, recipient name, and TZS amount. ChapSmart calculates the sats, you confirm, and M-Pesa arrives in seconds.

- **No registration required** for users — just enter details and send
- **Live exchange rate** from Binance/OKX
- **2.2% fee** — transparent, no hidden costs
- **Vodacom M-Pesa** (074, 075, 076 prefixes)
- **2,500 to 1,000,000 TZS** per transaction
